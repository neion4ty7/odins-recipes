Pizza

Description

A succulant homemade pepperoni pizza that will surprise the whole family on a budget. Traditional recipe passed down for generation whilst being value focused and health concious.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarela
  • Pepparoni
  • Basil
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Knead dough
  3. Crush tomatoes into paste
  4. Add mozerella, basil and pepparonis to taste
  5. Cook in oven
